This Privacy Policy has been created in order to disclose our information collection, use and disclosure practices for the app Randomology ("app").

Randomology is an app for Android phones that allows placing phone calls and the sending of SMS messages via your mobile carrier. The content of your SMS messages are only ever transported via your mobile carrier. All communication in this respect is between your mobile device and your mobile carrier. We are not involved in this communication and as such do not collect any personal information.

The app requests access to the phone contacts for the sole purpose of selecting a random contact for witch to place the call or send the SMS. This data is not collected or disclosed.

You accept that you may be charged by your carrier for these actions.

We use Google Ads so it might collect certain non-personal information in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you.

We use Google Analytics where we only collect non-personal information like crash reports or actions selected in the app.

We may also use certain non-personal information to internally analyze, develop and improve our products.

We take seriously the security of all information associated with the app and take all reasonable precautions to protect this information.
